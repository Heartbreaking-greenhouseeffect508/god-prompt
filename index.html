Frequently Asked Questions

Is the God Prompt completely free?

Yes. The God Prompt is 100% open-source and free to use. It is maintained by the community on GitHub under the MIT License.

Which AI models does this work with?

It is highly optimized for flagship tier models including OpenAI's GPT-4, Anthropic's Claude 3 (Opus and Sonnet), and Google's Gemini Advanced. It also works exceptionally well with open-source models like Llama 3.

Can I modify the prompt for my specific workflow?

Absolutely. The God Prompt is designed as a modular framework. We encourage you to fork the repository, tweak the personality traits, or add specific constraints that fit your exact use case.
